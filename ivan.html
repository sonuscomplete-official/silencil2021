Ivan Rutherford

Ivan Rutherford is coaching voice
  • Voice

Ivan Rutherford has performed the role of Jean Valjean in Les Miserables over 2,300 times on Broadway and national tours. He was honored with the role during the 10th anniversary company in New York and played alongside Andrea McArdle (original Annie) at the Ogunquit Playhouse in Maine, and Lea Salonga on Broadway in the 20th anniversary Les Miz revival. Ivan was also nominated for Best Actor for his portrayal of Valjean at the First Annual National Broadway Theatre Awards, held at the New York Supper Club in NYC. Some other favorite roles include The Caliph in Kismet, Tony in Westside Story, Lt. Joe Cable in South Pacific (in which he won a Drama-Logue Award) and Officer Charles Lightoller in the Broadway production of Titanic.

Ivan has been coaching Voice for the last 20 years and has more than 30 years of experience in the Industry.  Ivan coaches a range of participants from absolute beginners all the way up to some of the biggest music acts in the industry.

Ivan offers Voice lessons on the platform and also offers package deals once you complete your first Voice lesson.  All voice participants will send Ivan a video of themselves performing a song of their choice before their first session.  Ivan will analyze your video and come to your session prepared.  Whether you’re beginner, advanced, pre-professional, or professional, Ivan Rutherford will help you excel in your music career!

Schedule your first session using his 30-minute private coaching calendar! Follow ups and continued support will be offered after completion of your first session.  If you have any questions, email info@icoachdance.com.
